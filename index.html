About Us

UNICEF has been providing children, and their families, essential and urgent support towards more fulfilling lives

Help us make a better world for childrens

Our community of supporters, like yourself, has been an integral part of this joyful journey of giving children the best start in life.

For 75 years, we have been working relentlessly to safeguard their rights and growth. We work closely with the Government of India and our partners to improve the lives of the most vulnerable children with long-lasting interventions.
